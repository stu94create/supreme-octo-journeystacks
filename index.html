Add to Home Screen to protect your data
In Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Make sure it says Add to Home Screen and not Add Bookmark. This tells iOS to treat Stacks like a real app and makes it much less likely to clear your data.
⚠️
Back up — your data can be lost
Your library is stored on this device only. If Safari clears its data your library will be gone with no way to recover it. Go to Settings → Data & Backup → Backup. This shares a backup file you can save to iCloud Drive or email to yourself. Do this regularly.

Data & Backup
📱 About your data

Your library is stored on this device in Safari. It stays safe as long as you don't clear Safari's website data in iPhone Settings.

Save this app to your home screen — tap Share in Safari then Add to Home Screen (not Add Bookmark). This makes data loss much less likely.

Back up regularly using the Backup button above. Tap it and use the share sheet to save to iCloud Drive, Notes, or email it to yourself. If the share sheet does not appear, use the Copy All button to copy your backup text and paste it somewhere safe.
