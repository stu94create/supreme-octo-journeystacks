Add to Home Screen to protect your data
In Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Make sure it says Add to Home Screen and not Add Bookmark. This tells iOS to treat Stacks like a real app and makes it much less likely to clear your data.
⚠️
Back up — your data can be lost
Your library is stored on this device only. If Safari clears its data your library will be gone with no way to recover it. Go to Settings → Backup and save the file to iCloud Drive or email it to yourself. Do this regularly.

📱 About your data

Your library is stored on this device in Safari. It stays safe as long as you don't clear Safari's website data in iPhone Settings.

For the best experience, save this app to your home screen — tap Share in Safari then Add to Home Screen. On the next screen make sure it says Add to Home Screen and not Add Bookmark. This opens it full screen and makes data loss much less likely.

Back up regularly using the Backup button above and store the file in iCloud Drive or email it to yourself.
